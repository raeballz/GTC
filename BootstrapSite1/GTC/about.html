About Us

Meet the team! With over 60 years combined experience, we're sure that we can find your perfect product.

Opened in June 2013 we are an independant supplier of Porcelain, Ceramic and Mosaic tiles to both retail and trade customers, we also carry a full range of adhesives, grouts, edgings and tools.
