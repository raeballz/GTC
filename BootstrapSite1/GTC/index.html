Welcome to Glasgow Tile Center

Established 2012 we are a supplier of fine quality wall, floor and contract tiles from the latest ceramic, porcelain and mosaic ranges from the UK, Spain and Italy.

Our highly experienced staff bring over 60 years collective experience in the tile industry. Our showroom and distribution centre is based in East Kilbride and offer quality products along with excellent service at an affordable price.

We provide tiles to suit various projects from the smallest of bathrooms to housebuilding and commercial projects.
